Zero Inflation, One Nation

Zion is a new economic model that directly challenges fiat currency. We believe currency should work for the people, not against them. Zion is deflationary by design, applying the same supply and demand principles that give value to gold. By utilizing the power of blockchain, market makers, gas fees, and automated liquidity strategies, Zion redefines wealth preservation in the digital age.

Roadmap

Our roadmap includes strategic phases such as community building, influencer campaigns, education rollouts, liquidity provisioning, and governance evolution. We plan to integrate staking, implement decentralized voting, and continually develop utility for Zion.

Tokenomics

Zion launches with an initial supply of 1 trillion tokens. Through a transparent and strategic burn cycle, we aim to reduce supply to 100 billion. Our burn strategy is paired with scheduled buys to create consistent liquidity pressure and increase long-term value. Every transaction strengthens the system through well-distributed fees and deflationary mechanisms.
